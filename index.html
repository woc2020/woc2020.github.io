About the Event

Weekend of Code, the annual technical event of MNNIT, Allahabad is up with its 2k20 edition! The enthralling 10 days long event consists of Devjam and Codestart. Devjam aims at providing a glimpse of the world of development to our freshmen while Codestart is a coding event for our freshmen and sophomores. The sole purpose of this event is to provide participants with hands-on experience and exposure along with proper guidance. Get ready for an adventurous journey of learning, innovating and exploring new technologies.

  • 10 Days dedicated to coding and development
  • Meet Developers and Coders from your batch
  • Get Inspired by Amazing Mentors
  • Make project in any language you want
  • Test your coding skills with 5 hour insomaniac event "CodeStart"
  • Completely Remote Edition
